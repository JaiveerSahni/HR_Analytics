# HR_Analytics

# 🎯 Objective

To analyze employee engagement, performance, salary distribution, absenteeism, and recruitment effectiveness in order to identify patterns influencing workforce productivity.

# 📂 Dataset
The dataset includes information about:
- Employee salary
- Department
- Performance scores
- Engagement survey scores
- Employee satisfaction
- Absenteeism
- Recruitment sources
- Special project involvement

❓ Key Business Questions
- Who are the employees with the highest salaries?
- Which employees require attention due to low performance?
- What are the absence patterns across employees?
- How many employees are involved in special projects?
- What is the department-wise salary distribution?
- Which recruitment sources are most effective?
- What are the trends in employee performance scores?
- How does employee satisfaction vary over time?
- What is the average engagement survey score by job position?

🔍 Analysis Performed
- Data Cleaning & Preprocessing
- Univariate Analysis
- Bivariate Analysis
- Department-wise salary comparison
- Trend Analysis of Performance & Satisfaction
- Recruitment Source Effectiveness
- Engagement Score Distribution
- Absenteeism Insights

📊 Key Insights
- Identified salary concentration across departments

- Highlighted employees with low engagement and performance scores

- Analyzed recruitment channels contributing maximum hires

- Observed correlation between engagement, satisfaction, and performance

- Evaluated absenteeism trends affecting productivity
